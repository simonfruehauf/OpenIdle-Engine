# Notes

You are a spore. You must consume organic matter, spread hyphae, and mutate to survive a hostile surface world.

## Mechanics

Growth Cycles (Progress Bars):

Task: "Digest Compost".

Type: type: 'normal' with progressRequired: 60 and autoRestart: true.

Reward: Every 60 seconds, you get Nutrients.

## Mutations (Slots & Items)

Slots: membrane, spore_sac, root_system.

Items: "Acidic Coating" (Equip to Membrane).

Effect: The item provides a modify_task_yield_pct buff to the "Digest Compost" task.

Exclusive Evolution: Use exclusiveWith in Actions. You can choose "Evolution: Photosynthesis" (locks you into surface play) OR "Evolution: Deep Root" (locks you into underground play).
